Our History

Started in 2021, During the pandemic, The Wholesale Apparels Shop is an online platform created as side project by Adeeb akhtar

This Website serves as an online shopping platform for an as of yet hypothetical Shop where clothes are bought in bulk for affordable prices with greater emphasis on quality. Individual items can be also bought separately here at the best market prices.

WAS is owned by the joint owners named Atiqur Rahman and Adeeb Akhtar. It gained much popularity because of its quality services
